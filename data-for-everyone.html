Abstract

The most important thing in Computer Science : "Data and functions" - Rich Hickey. From the user perspective also, data is the only thing he can own and understand.

The more the programmer give to the end-user the more powerful the software is. Because the power not resides anymore inside the code.

I will present some real world examples to demonstrate how clojure can help people to build their own application. Let's make data really open.

Purpose

This process are called also DSL. Naturally clojure has many examples like cascalog c2po enlive…

Here some other examples of what can be treated as data.

The ultimate goal is to release the data from IT big companies and give it back to the user. Let's have real open data.

Music as Data

Describe a complete piece of music as plain data. Play it and record it. Easy to share just plain data.

World Wide Web as Data

Most web sites provide one single view to their data : HTML pages. These HTML pages contain text, image, video, audio.

Some web sites provide API, but it can never match what user wants.

Give scraping capabilities to the user : let him choose the data he wants from the WWW to build his own feed aggregators.

dataQuery

Describe your data sources (DB, excel, xml, csv files) in terms of dimensions and measures. And then join, project, aggregates as you want. https://github.com/maxrzepka/dataQuery

Photogram

Make image maipulation accessible, give Image Magick to everyone. No need of complex GUI just express what you want.

Book as Data

Separate the content of your slides from the presentation (keynote , impress.js , pdf …).

The Separation of concerns is a good practice for the programmer as well as for the user.

Any book is a graph of smaller pieces (text, image, code, sound, video, graph). Based on this graph, you can decide in which format you want see your book. Having this clear separation makes multi-language support simpler.

Application as Data

The next step is to let him own his application : services like storage, authentification, HTML template are just functions on data.

Users create their own applications because data runs code.

Let the user have his own IT company.
